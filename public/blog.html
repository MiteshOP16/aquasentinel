Predicting Beach Safety with AI & Ocean Data

AquaSentinel: Enhancing Coastal Tourism Safety through Predictive Intelligence and Real-Time Alerts

As coastal tourism continues to rise in popularity, so does the risk associated with unmonitored beach visits. From unpredictable weather to sudden natural disasters like tsunamis and flooding, beachgoers often face threats that can be prevented with timely awareness and data-driven decisions.

AquaSentinel is an innovative system that uses machine learning, real-time data, and automated email alerts to predict and inform users about beach safety.

Why We Need Beach Safety Intelligence

  • High wave heights
  • Strong undercurrents
  • Tide surges
  • Severe weather conditions
  • Natural disasters (tsunami, flooding)

System Workflow

Prediction Model (Random Forest)

The Random Forest classifier uses 9 input features such as:

  • Wave Height, Direction, Period
  • Sea Surface Temperature, Currents, Tides
  • Wind Speed and Direction
  • Weather (Sunny, Rainy, Stormy)

The output is a simple classification: Safe or Unsafe

Email Alert Feature

When INCOIS issues an alert, users registered to a matching beach region receive an instant email. This is handled using Nodemailer in the backend.
